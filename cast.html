CAST

The characters in the 1977 movie "Titanic" are loosely based on real-life survivors and victims of the sinking, but Jack and Rose's relationship was mostly fictional.

James Cameron's film is comprised of a renown, iconic cast and crew, many of which received accolades for their performances including Oscar wins for best picture and director, as well as winning 11 of 14 Academy Award nominations.
